Notes
  • Bloom periods on this page are from Theodore S. Cochrane, Kandis Elliot, Claudia S. Lipke, “Prairie Plants of the University of Wisconsin-Madison Arboretum”, University of Wisconsin Press, 2008 or from the webpages of the Wisconsin State Herbarium, University of Wisconsin-Madison.
  • The images on this page are licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial-ShareAlike 3.0 Unported License.
  • All of the listed plants can be found in the county (northern one third or so) portion of Pheasant Branch Conservancy, but the photo locations vary (mostly) amongst Pheasant Branch, Owen Park, and the UW Arboretum.
  • This site, including images and accompanying text, was created and is maintained by Tom Klein. I'm an amateur at plant identification: plant names associated with pictures on this site are my best effort at identification, but my work has not been verified by any expert. If you believe I have misidentified a plant please email pbcflora@gmail.com.
